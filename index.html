About Me

I am a graduating Senior at Emory University double majoring in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics. I am currently working on an Honor Thesis on county-level pandemic forecasting using multiple data sources advise by Prof. Li Xiong. I am also an undergraduate research assisstant in Prof. Liang Zhao's lab, working on the deep controllable generation.

I grew up in Beijing, China, and went to high school in San Diego, CA. On my free time, I love playing basketball, snowboarding, and cooking.
